About

The Academic Council, under the Studentsâ€™ Gymkhana of Indian Institute of Technology Indore, has come up with the research conclave to put together an event to foster research practices, strengthen our ties with the world of science and technology and aspiring people from all over the world.

Objectives

The objective of the Conclave is an exhibition of the ongoing research activities by research groups of different educational institutions across countries. Thus providing a platform for exchange of thoughts between distinguished guests and participants from different cultural and scientific backgrounds, facilitating industry and academia interaction with emphasis on the technological requirements. The Conclave aims to inspire and encourage the undergraduate and postgraduate student community to pursue career in research. Research conclave has become a veritable platform to nurture the young minds towards research, innovation and entrepreneurship and intends to bring the undivided attention of the students towards both industries and academia to redress the academic research challenges, concerns of the entire student community and upcoming entrepreneurs around the globe. It is a forum to harness innovative, minds of current society by taking innovations made in research to their actual implementation in the industries. Research conclave aims to be the catalyst which builds leads through holistic, transformable and innovative ideas.

Theme

"Emerging Technologies for Sustainable Future"

Our topic of interest will revolve around the following domains:

Smart and Intelligent Vehicles

The quest for lower emissions have turned focus towards the development of electric, autonomous vehicles.

Smart and Intelligent Vehicles

Artificial Intelligence Machine Learning

Automating technologies saves time and energy. AI and ML allows the automation using the rigorous prediction power using existing data.

Artificial Intelligence Machine Learning

Climate and Remote Sensing

The issue of global warming isn't new. Various advancements on the topic of climate change are of hot interest.

Climate and Remote Sensing

Data and Computational Techniques

Computational and Data techniques allows to simulate the physics, which allows faster and safer analysis of the particular field.

Data and Computational Techniques

Energy and Sustainability

The need to have a sustainable system is becoming an essential requirement, and energy saving, conservation is one major part.

Energy and Sustainability

Advanced Materials

To move towards a green future, high efficiency materials are being studied to harvest more energy.

Advanced Materials

Agriculture and Healthcare

Agriculture and Healthcare
