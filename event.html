About

The Academic Council, under the Students’ Gymkhana of Indian Institute of Technology Indore, has come up with the research conclave to put together an event to foster research practices, strengthen our ties with the world of science and technology and aspiring people from all over the world.

Objectives

The objective of the Conclave is an exhibition of the ongoing research activities by research groups of different educational institutions across countries. Thus providing a platform for exchange of thoughts between distinguished guests and participants from different cultural and scientific backgrounds, facilitating industry and academia interaction with emphasis on the technological requirements. The Conclave aims to inspire and encourage the undergraduate and postgraduate student community to pursue career in research. Research conclave has become a veritable platform to nurture the young minds towards research, innovation and entrepreneurship and intends to bring the undivided attention of the students towards both industries and academia to redress the academic research challenges, concerns of the entire student community and upcoming entrepreneurs around the globe. It is a forum to harness innovative, minds of current society by taking innovations made in research to their actual implementation in the industries. Research conclave aims to be the catalyst which builds leads through holistic, transformable and innovative ideas.

Theme

Emerging Technologies for Sustainabale Future.

Benefits

To foster interest in Research through participation. All the research teams will get an opportunity to present their works and projects. The event's momentum will drive more people towards pursuing research and will foster sharing knowledge within the student community.

  • An arena where students can gain knowledge from key people in emerging industries. This can help students get better acquainted with the industry scenario and find their passion.
  • Collaboration between the various departments would be beneficial for all the students. All the outside students can also participate in these engaging events and expand their horizons due to interdisciplinary activities.
  • Provide you with a platform to showcase your work and talent to the entire world.
